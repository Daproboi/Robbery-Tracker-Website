XP Curve Strategy

Season 31 uses a 1.2x exponential curve. This means Level 9 to 10 requires significantly more XP than Level 1 to 2. To finish the season without a pass, you must complete at least 85% of your daily contracts.

Daily Cap Intel

The daily XP cap resets exactly at 8 PM EST (00:00 UTC). You can earn up to 100 XP per day from standard contracts. Weekly challenges do not count toward this daily limit.
