Understanding the XP System

Season 31 features a dynamic progression system where the XP requirement for each level scales exponentially. By utilizing a 1.2x curve factor, the difficulty increases as you approach Level 10, making the final stretch significantly more challenging than the initial levels.

Players can earn XP through two main methods: completing daily contracts within the 100 XP daily cap and finishing weekly challenges. To ensure you reach the final tier within the 45-day season window, consistent daily participation is required. Missing consecutive days may necessitate a Season Pass to reach the grand prize in time.
