Effective Date: February 24, 2026

1. Information Collection

Jailbreak Hub is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not require accounts, email addresses, or Roblox credentials to use our tools. Any data you enter into our calculators or trackers is processed locally on your device or used temporarily to provide the requested service.

We use LocalStorage to save your preferences, such as your calculated trade values or robbery tracker progress. This data remains on your browser and is not transmitted to our servers.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
