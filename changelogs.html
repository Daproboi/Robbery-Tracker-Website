Chronological history of the Hub and Jailbreak updates.

Feb 20 2026 // VERSION 1539
Season 31: Freestyle

Season 31: Freestyle

  • New Vehicle: JBX-0031. Featuring 3 rocket thrusters. Your central thruster fires all the time, and left/right thrusters help you steer at higher speeds. Features a one-of-one skin, working hatch door, and seating for 3! Plus a unique thruster sound. Free at Level 10.
  • New Items: Theory and Quicksilver Rims, Freestyle Tire Sticker, Voltline Tires, Skyline, Hologram and Stinger Scythe Wings, Digital RGB Vehicle Skin, and Dark Matter Drift.

Tier-5 Safe Revamp

  • New Vehicle: Bluebird Dragster! Featuring absurd acceleration and power, but incredibly weak steering and brakes. Plan your turns far ahead and learn to drive this beast for the best speeds. Legendary.
  • New Items: Aeroblob and Monsterteeth Rims, Rainboom Drift, SuperAttack and Cassette Wings, Crazy Driver Wall Item, and Closed Circuit Tires.
  • Cassette features subtle animations when you play your Radio while driving!
  • Retired: Cheeseburger and Tesseract Rims, and Tesla Wing. Obtain these items through trading.
  • Moved Manta from Legendary to Epic. Moved Electronic Vehicle Skin from Epic to Ultra-Rare.
  • Moved to Garages: BoominSpeaker, Skater, and Dragonbreath Wings, EvilEye Rims.

More Content & Fixes

  • New “Bunny Bed” Floor Item for homes and “Birdtail” Wing for the Bluebird Dragster.
  • New “Hypershift” easter egg for speedometers…
  • Added Tire Style and Tire Stickers to Trailblazer and Semi vehicles.
  • Black Widow is now dual-wheel in the rear with support for Tire Style and Tire Stickers.
  • Updated Spoiler positioning and sizing for Shell Mark 5, Overdrive, Torero, BicycleRack, and Carbonara.
  • Keepinventory now remembers item layout and fixed for Nerf Gun players.
  • Updated Cinematic Camera code. You can now camera track players with / in Private Servers.
  • Added “clear” command to console and ability to copy command text.
  • Removed “Team Full” restrictions for Private Servers.
  • Fixed bumpy map terrain, world gaps, and Military Forcefield issues.
  • Fixed Train sounds harshly cutting in/out and lowered volume.
  • Performance improvements to Garage loading and weapon name cleanup.
