Jan 23 2026 // VERSION 1530
Robbery Revamp: Bank Truck

Bank-Truck Revamp

  • New truck delivery zones.
  • Bank Truck now uses Bank Cash UI for nearby Police and participating players.
  • Bank Truck starts at 11,000 cash and now depletes as you drive.
  • Players now receive the full truck cash amount when collecting.
  • Police within 200 studs of an active Bank Truck robbery will see the Robbery UI.
  • Upon delivery, Police within 100 studs will be automatically awarded the full amount.
  • Upon theft, Nearby criminals have can walk inside and collect the full amount.
  • Upon theft or delivery, the truck now screeches to a halt.
  • Players now have 20 seconds to collect their reward upon theft or delivery.
  • Removed the 2 Police starter requirement.
  • New garage with closed sign, lights, and an "arrests required" counter.
  • New truck design, refined and with more detail, and a steering wheel.
  • New sound effects for engine start, tire screech, and more.
  • Faster, Semi Truck Performance engine tune.

More

  • New "Purchase Vehicle" UI.
  • New Bank Floor: Deductions V2. Revolving escape doors change how you rob and escape.
  • Bank Floor: Underwater escape has been updated.
  • Bank Floor: Presidential now has a larger vault and easier collection.
  • Bank Floor: Basement now has improved Glider prevention and better head room.
  • Bank trapdoors now use our latest door technology.
  • Updates to Haptics on Controller! Controllers can disable Haptics in settings.
  • World Markers now account for map height and appear on fullscreen Minimap.
  • World Marker visuals have been slightly updated.
  • Improved camera for vehicle turrets.
  • Popped tire vehicles now roll on their rims instead of floating.
  • Added inflation sound to popped tires and custom tire pop sound to Volt.
  • Added Start Engine sound to Semi vehicles.
  • Added new player "onboard funnels" to better track new users.
  • Added the top secret, dev only, nuke vehicle. Stay tuned.
  • Fixed home raids, UFO HyperChrome, and C4 sticking to your character.
  • Fixed camera shake being FPS dependent and road bollard collisions.
  • Fixed players getting trapped behind opening Presidential Vault door.
  • Fixed avatar accessory first person camera bug and Bank aiming bugs.
  • Fixed "arrests off" command not disabling Bank Busts.
  • Fixed arrest after breakout sending players to Prison too quickly.
  • Fixed Helicopter Ropes colliding with cars when empty.
  • Removed the old Bank Truck notification.
Jan 16 2026 // VERSION 1528
Rising Bank Revamp

New Rising City Bank

  • New exterior design with exterior neon lighting (when open) and OG inspired interior.
  • Floor: Upper Management - Built from scratch; shortened ladder; new Exit Penthouse/roof door; vault view change; widened halls.
  • Floor: Corridor - Modified design; new zipline entry; "Jail Gate" door for tactics; expanded side rooms; updated lighting.
  • Floor: Classic - True 1:1 visual of 2017’s OG floor; expanded exit room; original lighting.
  • Floor: Underwater - Built from scratch to remove swimming; extended main room/stairs; larger escape.
  • Floor: The Mint - Widened halls; new cover; fewer lasers; new double escape doors; rapid conveyor belts.
  • All floors require less dynamite and cash collection is ~25% faster.
  • New rapid ticking cash UI visual.

Crater City Bank Updates

  • Removed: Deductions and Blue Floor.
  • Floor: Basement - Widened entry; simplified escape; fewer lasers; new glass walls; new stairwell.
  • Floor: Presidential Vault - New zipline; increased head room; widened hall; halved laser speed; simplified escape.

Map & More

  • Roblox Brick is back! Updated Rising City Tunnel; NoVehicle zone at Bank trapdoor.
  • New sun GeographicLatitude (40) for improved lighting.
  • New Oil Rig escape: Roof Ziplines with free cruiser spawn below.
  • New “Lowest” sort in Garages (new default).
  • New Controller Haptics (v1) and improved high-speed driving.
  • Reduced prison punch box requirement from 10 to 8.
  • Equip items by clicking anywhere in the weapon shop window.
  • Criminals no longer get a pistol when broken out of handcuffs.
  • Bank “Place Dynamite” prompts hidden once initiated; "Closed" signs support translations.
  • Bank escape doors stay open longer; Bank-Truck spawning and icons improved.
  • Removed Rising Penthouse roof hatch; revamped UFO Hyperchrome effects.
  • Jeep spawn added to Military Base.
  • Private Server commands: towing toggle and op list functionality.
  • Smoother rejoining; fixed ATV invisibility, Jewelry roof no-clip, and mansion doors.
  • Fixed racetrack minimap, home furniture teleporting, and garage previews.
