Jan 16 2026 - Version 1528
Rising Bank Revamp
New Rising City Bank
  • New exterior design with exterior neon lighting (when open) and OG inspired interior.
  • Floor: Upper Management - Built from scratch to fit the new Rising Bank exterior. Shortened exit ladder. New Exit Penthouse with new roof exit door option. Vault placement change with a clear, defendable, view down the room. Widened hallways and increased headspace.
  • Floor: Corridor - Modified design with new zipline entry option, better cover, and widened halls. New “Jail Gate” door, optional, for quicker entry and exit to the vault. Expanded side rooms for better cover and team interaction. Updated lighting and visuals.
  • Floor: Classic (Previously Classic Remastered) - Now a true, near 1:1, visual of 2017’s Jailbreak original Bank floor. Expanded exit room for better team interaction. Original lighting and visuals.
  • Floor: Underwater - Familiar design, but built from scratch to remove swimming. Extended main room, added stairs. Larger escape room.
  • Floor: The Mint - Widened hallways and new cover options. Fewer lasers and updated main room leading to the vault. New double escape door design. New escape room with rapid moving cash conveyor belts.
  • All floors now require less dynamite on vault doors, speeding up robberies.
  • Cash collection is now ~25% faster.
  • New, rapid, ticking cash UI visual.
Crater City Bank Updates
  • Removed: Deductions and Blue Floor.
  • Floor: Basement - Widened entry staircase. Simplified escape. Fewer lasers and new glass walls to better team interaction. New stairwell and extended hall in the Vault.

Updated Vault room design with clear markings for where to collect cash. Halved the speed of the lasers in the Vault. Simplified the escape, and flipped the ladder direction 180 degrees.
